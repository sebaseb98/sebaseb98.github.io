Radon Transform

The Radon transform is a mathematical transformation used in tomography to reconstruct an image from its projections.

For more information about the Radon transform, you can visit the following resources:

  • Wikipedia - Radon Transform
  • Wolfram MathWorld - Radon Transform

On this page, we want to make the Radon transform a bit more intuitive and give a little playground that helps understanding this complex operation.
